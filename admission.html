About B.Tech Admissions

Gujarat Board Class 12 and state-level engineering entrance exam result 2021 can be expected in July. After the announcement of these results, ACPC Gujarat will initiate the process for B.Tech counselling process. B. Tech aspirants of Gujarat must note that clearing entrance test is mandatory to secure B.Tech admission in Gujarat. 5% of seats in all engineering colleges across the state are applicable for students who apply under Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) scheme. In all, there is an adequate number of options for engineering aspirants in terms of the number of engineering colleges across the state.

ACPC has the authority to fill 100% of seats in Government Engineering Colleges and 50% of seats in Private Engineering Colleges across the state.

Engineering (B.Tech/ B.E) Admission and Selection Procedure

Gujarat B.Tech/ B.E admission process is completely online, and the admission committee for professional courses is responsible for conducting admission process for engineering courses in Gujarat. The admission procedure for B.Tech courses in Gujarat involves the following stages –

Merit Preparation:
The admission authority prepares a merit list of candidates who have submitted an online application form for Gujarat B.Tech counselling process. The merit list prepared as follows –

  • While preparing the GUJCET merit list, 60% weightage will be applied to percentile scored by candidates in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics subjects of Class 12.
  • Secondly, 40% of weightage will be applied to percentile scored by candidates in GUJCET exam.
  • By considering the above factors, the admission authority prepares a Common Merit List for Gujarat B.Tech admissions.

    • Documents Required for Admission

    The candidates must produce the following documents at the time of admission –

  • Class 10 &12 Certificate
  • GUJCET Mark Sheet
  • TC (Transfer Certificate)
  • Passport Size Photographs ( at least 4)
  • Income Certificate
  • Printout of Registration Form (the counselling registration form)
